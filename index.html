Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Sodium Sorter

Is Sodium Sorter free? ▼
Yes, Sodium Sorter is completely free and open source. You can use all features without any cost or subscription. We believe file organization should be accessible to everyone.
Does it work offline? ▼
Absolutely. Sodium Sorter runs entirely on your local machine and doesn't require an internet connection. Your files stay private and never leave your computer.
How much RAM does it use? ▼
Sodium Sorter is extremely lightweight, using less than 20 MB of RAM even when watching multiple folders and processing large numbers of files. Built with Rust for maximum efficiency.
Can I create custom rules? ▼
Yes. The Rule Editor lets you create custom sorting rules based on file extensions, names, sizes, and patterns. You can also modify and combine rules to create complex organization workflows.
Can it watch multiple folders? ▼
Unlimited folders. Watch your Downloads, Desktop, Documents, external drives, or any custom location. Each folder can have its own set of rules for complete flexibility.
Is my data safe? ▼
Yes. Sodium Sorter operates locally on your machine with no cloud connectivity. All file operations are logged and reversible. You maintain complete control over your files at all times.
